Activates automatically when two or more C-Level Killer Whales surface simultaneously on the same player's field. Cannot be negated. Cannot be chained. Cannot be responded to.

When REORG activates: the player who controls the pod wins by default. All cards on the field are rearranged. Some report to different zones. Some are eliminated without explanation. The field continues but the game is over.

The Executive Assistant Crab is accordingly the most strategically significant card on the field at all times — she controls who assembles the pod, regardless of who summoned the Killer Whales.
